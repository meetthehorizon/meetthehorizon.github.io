Yato Kami: A Broke God

Yato is a minor war god with a mysterious and violent past, once feared for slaying both gods and humans with the help of cursed 'nora' spirits. Now, he dreams of becoming the most revered—and wealthy—god in the modern world, offering his divine services for just five yen.
